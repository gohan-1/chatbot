SHIPPING INFORMATION
====================

SHIPPING OPTIONS:
1. Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days - $4.99
2. Express Shipping: 2-3 business days - $12.99
3. Overnight Shipping: Next business day - $24.99
4. Free Shipping: Available on orders over $75

SHIPPING DESTINATIONS:
- United States: All shipping options available
- Canada: Standard and Express only (7-14 business days)
- International: Standard shipping only (10-21 business days)

PROCESSING TIME:
- Orders placed before 2 PM EST: Processed same day
- Orders placed after 2 PM EST: Processed next business day
- Weekend orders: Processed on Monday
- Holiday delays: Check website for holiday schedule

TRACKING:
- Tracking number sent via email within 24 hours
- Track your order at: www.oursite.com/track
- SMS tracking updates available (opt-in)

SHIPPING ADDRESS:
- Can be changed before order ships
- Contact us immediately if address is wrong
- We're not responsible for incorrect addresses

DELIVERY ISSUES:
- Lost packages: Contact us within 14 days
- Damaged items: Take photos and contact us immediately
- Wrong item received: Keep item, contact us for correct item

INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING:
- Customs fees: Customer responsibility
- Delivery time: 10-21 business days
- Restricted items: Check country-specific restrictions

QUESTIONS ABOUT SHIPPING:
Q: How long does shipping take?
A: Standard shipping is 5-7 business days. Express is 2-3 days, Overnight is next day.

Q: Do you ship internationally?
A: Yes, we ship to most countries. Standard shipping takes 10-21 business days.

Q: Can I change my shipping address?
A: Yes, contact us before your order ships and we can update it.

Q: What if my package is lost?
A: Contact us within 14 days and we'll investigate and send a replacement.
