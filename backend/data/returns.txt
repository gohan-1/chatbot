RETURNS AND REFUNDS POLICY
===========================

RETURN POLICY:
- Items can be returned within 30 days of purchase
- Items must be in original condition with tags attached
- Original receipt or order number required

REFUND PROCESS:
1. Contact customer service with your order number
2. Receive return authorization and shipping label
3. Package item securely and ship it back
4. Refund processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the item

REFUND METHODS:
- Original payment method: 5-7 business days
- Store credit: Instant upon approval
- Gift card purchases: Refunded as store credit only

EXCHANGES:
- Free exchanges available within 30 days
- Size/color exchanges: No restocking fee
- Different item exchanges: Price difference applies

NON-RETURNABLE ITEMS:
- Personalized or customized items
- Underwear and intimate apparel
- Gift cards
- Digital products
- Items damaged by customer

RETURN SHIPPING:
- Free returns for orders over $50
- Under $50: $5.99 return shipping fee
- Defective items: Free return shipping always

QUESTIONS ABOUT RETURNS:
Q: How long do returns take?
A: Processing takes 5-7 business days after we receive your item.

Q: Can I return sale items?
A: Yes, sale items can be returned within 30 days in original condition.

Q: What if I lost my receipt?
A: We can look up your order with your email address or order number.
