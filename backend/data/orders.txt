ORDER INFORMATION
=================

PLACING AN ORDER:
1. Add items to your cart
2. Review your cart and apply any coupons
3. Enter shipping and billing information
4. Select payment method
5. Review and confirm your order
6. Receive order confirmation email

ORDER STATUS:
- Pending: Order received, payment processing
- Processing: Order being prepared for shipment
- Shipped: Order has been shipped (tracking available)
- Delivered: Order has been delivered
- Cancelled: Order was cancelled

ORDER TRACKING:
- Check status: www.oursite.com/my-orders
- Login with email and order number
- Track shipments in real-time
- Email updates sent at each stage

ORDER MODIFICATIONS:
- Cancel order: Available within 1 hour of placement
- Change address: Contact us before order ships
- Add items: Place a new order
- Remove items: Cancel and reorder (if within 1 hour)

ORDER ISSUES:
- Wrong item: Contact us immediately, keep item until resolved
- Missing items: Check all packages, contact us if item missing
- Damaged items: Take photos, contact us for replacement
- Late delivery: Check tracking, contact us if significantly delayed

ORDER HISTORY:
- View all past orders in your account
- Reorder with one click
- Download receipts and invoices
- Track return status

BULK ORDERS:
- Orders over $500: Contact sales@oursite.com
- Business accounts: Special pricing available
- Volume discounts: Applied automatically at checkout

QUESTIONS ABOUT ORDERS:
Q: How do I track my order?
A: Use your order number and email to track at www.oursite.com/my-orders or check your email for tracking updates.

Q: Can I cancel my order?
A: Yes, you can cancel within 1 hour of placing the order. After that, contact us immediately.

Q: What if I receive the wrong item?
A: Contact us immediately. Keep the item and we'll send the correct one right away.

Q: How do I reorder something?
A: Log into your account, go to order history, and click "Reorder" on any past order.

Q: Can I change my order after placing it?
A: Changes can be made within 1 hour. After that, contact us and we'll try to help if the order hasn't shipped.

Q: What is my latest order?
A: To view your latest order, log into your account at www.oursite.com/my-orders. Your most recent orders appear at the top of the list. You can also check your email for the most recent order confirmation.

Q: Can I get all the order IDs?
A: Yes, you can view all your order IDs by logging into your account at www.oursite.com/my-orders. Your order history page shows all past orders with their order numbers. You can also find order IDs in your order confirmation emails.
